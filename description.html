How to Play

Hit: Hit refers to the specific number you are required to identify and press within the box that displays a range or set of numbers

Timer: The timer is a countdown that starts when you begin the game. You will have a total of 60 seconds to play the game. During this period, your objective is to select the specific number indicated by the 'hit' prompt within the numeric box. Once the 60 seconds elapse, the game will automatically conclude, and no further selections or actions will be allowed.

Score: The score section keeps track of your performance during the game. Your score increases each time you correctly select the number indicated by the 'hit' prompt. However, if you select an incorrect number, your score will decrease by 10 points as a penalty. The score dynamically updates in real-time based on your selections, reflecting your accuracy and progress throughout the game.
